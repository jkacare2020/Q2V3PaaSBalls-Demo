## 💡 What is this?

This project introduces a **Multi-Perspective AI System** that allows users to:

- Ask one question
- See how different personas (e.g., generational or expert) respond
- Compare values, trade-offs, and decision logic

Instead of one answer, users explore multiple ways of thinking.

---

## 🧠 Example

Question:

> Should I move to another city for a better job?

Output:

- 👴 Great Grandfather → Stability & family-first
- 👵 Great Grandmother → Relationship & emotional perspective
- 🧠 Modern Advisor → Growth & opportunity
